Alumni Finder

A website dedicated to connecting students with alumni in their prospective industries.

About

Created as a part of the Break Through Tech Initiative, this site allows students to build relationships with alumni in their field of interest. This platform is a great way for alumni to stay connected in the community and for students to network and learn about career opportunities and strategies. Oftentimes students find it hard to connect and network with others in their prospective industries. Our mission is to make it easier to find and deepen this connection.
